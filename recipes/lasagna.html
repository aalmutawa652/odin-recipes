Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a rich and comforting dish made by layering pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy cheese. It’s baked to perfection with a bubbly, golden top and flavorful filling.

Perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep, this dish never fails to satisfy. Whether you're a pasta lover or a cheese fanatic, lasagna hits the spot every time!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef with chopped onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and a pinch of salt.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
